Scrap Collection FAQs

How can I get the best scrap metal price per kg in Mumbai?

Prices vary daily based on the LME (London Metal Exchange). We offer rates based on current market transparency. For bulk quantities (above 1 ton), we provide premium pricing.

Do you provide weighbridge slips for industrial scrap?

Yes. For heavy loads, we use verified 100-ton weighbridges and provide digital slips to ensure 100% honesty in weight.

What is MPCB Form 10, and do you provide it?

Form 10 is a manifest for hazardous waste. As a compliant buyer, we provide necessary documentation for factory clearances to meet environmental norms.
